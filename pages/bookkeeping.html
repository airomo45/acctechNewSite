Wholesale Bookkeeping

 

If you are an Accountant and you are struggling with your huge number of clients bookkeeping or you have a big business and needs bulk bookkeeping, you can use our “Wholesale Bookkeeping” service and save your valuable time and money and, keep commitments with your customers.

Acctech is ready to help you and save your money and wants to create a good relationship among the chain; Acctech, you and your customers. Benefit will be made through subcontracting of bookkeeping data entry. In your busy tax season; it might be difficult for you to do regular bookkeeping while the business tax return is due by March 15. During this busy time we can help you in your bookkeeping data entry with a very nominal hourly rate or low contract price. Contact us today for more information.
