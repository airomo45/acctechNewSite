Accounting Services

 

Besides our wholesale bookkeeping service we also offer retail bookkeeping. We offer full service accounting for our retail customers; which includes creating invoice for our clients and send to their customers, recording payment received, record bill paid, write check and bookkeeping data entry,

We do the reconciliation of banks, customers and vendor accounts. We prepare financial statements, generate reports and analyze the reports and provide expert opinion our customers. We provide aging reports for the outstanding balance of customers payments. While using our service you can avoid unnecessary overdraft and NSF fees.

We help our customers in filing sales taxes in accordance with State law; calculating accurate sales tax for each zip code area and file on time. We help our customers to setup their accounting and payroll system in their own machine and help them to keep records, run payrolls and file taxes accurately and properly.
